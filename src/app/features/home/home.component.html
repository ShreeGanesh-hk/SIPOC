2016-11-18: New 2016 edition of the KEP 5&6

The KEP 5 (Key Elements Procedures) provides information on the Volvo Group’s Environmental Policy and its requirements for the supplier co-operation. The new edition makes it clearer what the Volvo Group requires from its suppliers and the compliant supplier self-assessment questionnaires are updated to better cater for the diversity among the suppliers.

The KEP 6 provides information on the Volvo Group’s requirements on ethical issues and appropriate business practices. As a member of the European Automotive Working Group on Supply Chain Sustainability and in line with general automotive industry practice, a common Self-assessment questionnaire has been developed to facilitate for the suppliers
